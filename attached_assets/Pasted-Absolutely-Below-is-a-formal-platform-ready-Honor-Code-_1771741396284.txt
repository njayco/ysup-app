# YsUp Campus Honor Code & Community Disclaimer

**Effective Date:** [Insert Date]

Welcome to YsUp Campus — a collaborative educational platform designed to promote independent learning, integrity, and positive community engagement.

By accessing or using YsUp Campus, you agree to uphold the following Honor Code and Community Standards.

---

## 1. Academic Integrity

YsUp Campus is designed to promote independent thinking and learning. Users agree:

* Not to use the platform to cheat on assignments, exams, quizzes, or academic evaluations.
* Not to request or share direct answers for graded coursework when doing so violates school policies.
* Not to impersonate another student or submit work on behalf of another person.
* Not to use AI tools within YsUp to bypass learning objectives or academic rules set by instructors.

YsUp encourages guidance, hints, collaboration, and critical thinking — not answer-sharing or academic dishonesty.

Violation of academic integrity may result in:

* Suspension or permanent removal from YsUp Campus
* Notification to educators or institutions (if applicable)

---

## 2. Copyright & Intellectual Property Protection

Users agree NOT to:

* Upload, share, distribute, or reproduce copyrighted materials without proper authorization.
* Share pirated textbooks, movies, music, software, exam banks, or proprietary academic materials.
* Post protected content that violates U.S. or international copyright law.

All users are responsible for ensuring the content they upload complies with copyright and intellectual property laws.

YsUp Campus reserves the right to:

* Remove infringing content immediately
* Suspend or terminate accounts
* Cooperate with legal authorities when required

---

## 3. Anti-Bullying & Respect Policy

YsUp Campus maintains a zero-tolerance policy for:

* Cyberbullying
* Harassment
* Hate speech
* Threats of violence
* Discrimination based on race, ethnicity, gender, religion, nationality, disability, or identity
* Encouraging in-person bullying or real-world harm

Users must treat others with dignity and respect in:

* Class Networks
* Direct messages
* Bulletin boards
* Public posts
* Online game sessions
* In-person interactions facilitated through YsUp

Any behavior that creates a hostile, intimidating, or unsafe environment may result in immediate account suspension or permanent ban.

---

## 4. Illegal Activity Prohibition

Users may not use YsUp Campus to:

* Engage in fraud or identity theft
* Sell or promote illegal goods or services
* Coordinate harmful or unlawful activities
* Distribute malware or attempt to hack the platform
* Circumvent platform safeguards

Illegal conduct may be reported to law enforcement authorities.

---

## 5. Responsible AI Usage

Where YsUp integrates AI tools:

* AI is designed to assist learning — not replace it.
* Users agree not to manipulate or exploit AI systems for misconduct.
* Users understand AI responses may not always be accurate and must use independent judgment.

---

## 6. Reporting Violations

If you witness behavior that violates this Honor Code, you are encouraged to report it through the platform’s reporting system.

Protecting the integrity of YsUp is a shared responsibility.

---

## 7. Platform Rights

YsUp Campus reserves the right to:

* Remove content
* Suspend or terminate accounts
* Restrict features
* Update this Honor Code at any time

Continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of updated terms.
